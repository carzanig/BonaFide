About BonaFide provider

Bonafide provider is system for measuring network performance between smartphones and Bonafide measurement servers distributed over the globe. This application can measure protocol-specific and random data exchange. With protocol-specific approach one can see how the network perform when using a specific protocol instead of measuring a simple random data exchange. This allows one to measure if data communication is shaped based on protocol.

Developers

The BonaFide system was designed by University of Zurich, Jacobs University Bremen, Technical University of Vienna, Universität der Bundeswehr München and developed by Tomas Ludrovan (TU Vienna, tomas@ludrovan.sk) and Vitali Bashko (Jacobs University Bremen). People participating on the underlaying Quality of Experience research: Christos Tsiaras, Anuj Sehgal, Sebastian Seeber and Andreas Ehringfeld.
